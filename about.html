About Leye Resources LTD

Your Trusted Partner in Premium Metal Trading

Our Story

Founded to serve the growing industrial demand in Nigeria and West Africa, Leye Resources LTD has grown to become a leading supplier of ferrous and non-ferrous metals across the region. Based in Akure, Ondo State, our journey has been built on a foundation of quality, reliability, and customer-centric service.

With a team of experienced professionals and modern infrastructure at Stateline FUTA Southgate, we have successfully served clients across manufacturing, construction, and industrial sectors throughout Nigeria. Our commitment to excellence has made us a trusted name in the West African metals trading industry.

Today, we continue to invest in technology, infrastructure, and talent to deliver superior products and services that exceed our customers' expectations while contributing to the development of Nigeria's industrial sector.
