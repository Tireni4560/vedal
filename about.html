About Vedal Resources

Your Trusted Partner in Premium Metal Trading

Our Story

Founded in 20XX, Vedal Resources has grown to become a leading supplier of ferrous and non-ferrous metals across India. Our journey has been built on a foundation of quality, reliability, and customer-centric service.

With a team of experienced professionals and a modern infrastructure, we have successfully served 500+ clients across manufacturing, construction, and industrial sectors. Our commitment to excellence has made us a trusted name in the industry.

Today, we continue to invest in technology, infrastructure, and talent to deliver superior products and services that exceed our customers' expectations.
