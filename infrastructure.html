Climate-Controlled Warehouses

Our facilities maintain optimal temperature and humidity levels to preserve product integrity and prevent corrosion. State-of-the-art security ensures round-the-clock protection of your inventory.

  • Total capacity: 10,000+ tons
  • Climate control: ±2°C temperature regulation
  • Humidity control: 40–60% RH
  • 24/7 monitoring and CCTV security
  • Fire suppression systems installed
  • ISO 9001:2015 certified operations

Quality Testing Laboratory

Advanced testing equipment ensures every batch meets industry standards and client specifications. Our accredited lab provides comprehensive quality assurance for all products.

  • Tensile strength testing equipment
  • Chemical composition analysis

  • Dimensional verification & tolerance checking
  • ISO/IEC 17025 accredited facility
  • Certified test reports for all shipments

Efficient Logistics & Distribution

Nationwide delivery network with modern fleet for timely, safe product transportation. Real-time tracking and insurance coverage ensure peace of mind for every shipment.

  • Fleet size: 50+ vehicles
  • Average delivery time: 3–7 days
  • Real-time GPS tracking available
  • Insurance coverage on all shipments
  • Professional handling teams
  • Nationwide network with hub locations across Nigeria
