Environmental Initiatives

Waste Management & Recycling

We have implemented comprehensive waste management programs to minimize landfill disposal and promote recycling. Metal scraps and by-products are recycled through certified recycling partners.

  • Metal scrap recycling program
  • Waste segregation at source
  • Partnerships with certified recyclers
  • Zero-waste-to-landfill initiatives

Energy Efficiency

We invest in energy-efficient equipment and practices to reduce our carbon footprint and operational costs.

  • LED lighting throughout facilities

Water is a precious resource. We implement water conservation measures and treatment systems to minimize usage and ensure safe disposal of wastewater.
